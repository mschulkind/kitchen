# User Profile: Dietary & Personal Preferences

This file documents the core personal and dietary preferences extracted from Phase 0 meal plans. It serves as the primary source of truth for the recommendation engine.

## 🥗 Dietary Goals & Macros

### 1. The "50-25-25" Architecture
- **Structure:** 50% non-starchy vegetables, 25% high-quality protein, 25% complex carbohydrates.
- **Strategy:** Use hefty vegetable sides (roasted, sautéed, or salads) to meet the 50% volume requirement.
- **Source:** `2026-01-25_hearty-stew/01-request.md`, `2026-01-19_turkish-soup-mini/01-request.md`

### 2. The "1:5:5:5" Macro Ratio
- **Ratio:** 1:5:5:5 (Fiber : Fat : Carb : Protein).
- **Focus:** High protein and fiber, moderate fat, controlled carbs.
- **Source:** `2026-02-11_shop-the-kitchen-low-gi/01-request.md`, `2026-03-04_stk-sirloin-steak/04-final-plan.md`

### 3. Low-Glycemic (Low-GI) Focus
- **Guidelines:** Minimize refined carbohydrates (white rice, white pasta, flour, sugar).
- **Starch Strategy:** Use "slow-release" starches only (sourdough, al dente farro/quinoa, pre-cooked/chilled skin-on potatoes).
- **Source:** `2026-03-04_stk-sirloin-steak/01-request.md`, `2026-01-19_turkish-soup-mini/01-request.md`

### 4. Fiber & Protein Minimums
- **Fiber-to-Carb Ratio:** Fiber must be at least 1/5th of total carbohydrates.
- **Protein:** Minimum 20-30g of protein per meal.
- **Source:** `2026-01-19_turkish-soup-mini/01-request.md`

## 👅 Flavor & Texture Profiles

### 1. Bold & Complex Layers
- **Preference:** High-depth flavors achieved via:
  - **Acid:** Liberal use of Lime/Lemon juice (e.g., Shirazi salad style) for brightening.
  - **Spices:** Blooming spices in fat and using bold aromatics (Aleppo pepper, cumin, garlic).
  - **Herbs:** Fresh herbs (garden-sourced when possible) to finish.
- **Source:** `2026-01-19_turkish-soup-mini/01-request.md`, `2026-03-04_stk-sirloin-steak/04-final-plan.md`

### 2. Texture & Umami
- **Texture Rule:** Meat must **never** be chewy or stringy (use velveting or hard searing).
- **Umami Strategy:** Prioritize Maillard reaction (roasting/searing) and high-flavor swaps (Capers/Sharp Cheddar instead of Olives).
- **Source:** `2026-01-19_turkish-soup-mini/01-request.md`

### 3. Spice Level
- **Level:** Likes spicy food, but "to a degree" (balanced, not overwhelming).
- **Source:** `process.md`
