## Ingredients

- 1 lb **Marinated Chicken Strips** (Thawed)
- 1 jar **Salsa** or homemade Tomato-Chipotle sauce (Blend tomatoes, chipotle in adobo, garlic)
- 1/2 **White Onion**, sliced
- **Shredded Cabbage** (Green or Red) - LOTS of it
- **Radishes**, sliced thin
- **Lime Wedges**

## For the Corn-Potato Salad

- 2 **Russet Potatoes**, boiled, cooled, and cubed
- 1 cup **Corn** (thawed)
- 2 tablespoons **Mayo** or Greek Yogurt
- 1 tablespoon **Lime Juice**
- **Chili Powder** to taste
- **Cilantro**, chopped

---

## Instructions

1.  **Cook Chicken:** In a skillet, sear the chicken strips over high heat until cooked through.
2.  **Sauce It:** Lower heat. Add sliced onions and cook until soft. Pour in your salsa/chipotle sauce and simmer for 5-10 minutes until chicken is coated and saucy.
3.  **Make Salad:** While chicken simmers, toss the cold potato cubes, corn, mayo, lime juice, chili powder, and cilantro in a bowl. Season with salt.
4.  **Assemble Bowl:** Start with a massive base of shredded cabbage. Top with the hot Chicken Tinga. Place a scoop of the cold Corn-Potato salad on the side. Garnish with radishes and extra lime.
