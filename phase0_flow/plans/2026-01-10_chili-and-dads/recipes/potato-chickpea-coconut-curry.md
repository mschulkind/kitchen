## Ingredients

- 2 large **Russet Potatoes**, unpeeled, scrubbed, and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
- 2 cans (15 oz) **Chickpeas**, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (13.5 oz) **Coconut Milk** (Full Fat)
- 1 bag (10 oz) **Fresh Spinach**
- 1 large **Onion**, diced
- 3 cloves **Garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **Fresh Ginger**, grated
- 1 tablespoon **Curry Powder** (or Red Curry Paste)
- 1 teaspoon **Turmeric**
- 1 cup **Vegetable Broth** (or Water)
- 1 tablespoon **Coconut Oil**
- **Lime Juice** to finish
- **Cilantro** for garnish

## For the Side

- **Roasted Cauliflower** (Use frozen fire-roasted bag or roast fresh florets at 400Â°F for 25 mins)

## Instructions

1.  **Aromatics:** Heat coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. SautÃ© onion for 5 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, curry powder, and turmeric. Cook for 1 minute until aromatic.
2.  **Simmer:** Add the potato cubes, chickpeas, coconut milk, and broth. Stir well. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until potatoes are tender.
3.  **Wilt Greens:** Stir in the spinach in batches until wilted into the sauce.
4.  **Finish:** Squeeze in the juice of half a lime. Taste and adjust salt.
