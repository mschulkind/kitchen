# Meal Plan Options

Here are 4 meal plan options tailored to your request for a **Chili Saturday** and a **Portable Sunday**, while strictly adhering to your **50-25-25** and **Texture** rules. ü•≥

All plans assume basic pantry staples (oil, spices, vinegars) are available.

## Option 1: The "Freezer Clear-Out" üßä
*Focuses heavily on using your existing frozen proteins.*

*   **Saturday (Tonight):** **Hearty Beef & Vegetable Chili.** Uses the **Chuck Stew Meat** (cut into small chunks) and **Beef Broth**. Loaded with bell peppers, carrots, and onions to hit the 50% veg target. Served with a side of chilled/reheated skin-on potatoes (resistant starch).
    *   *Kid Submeal:* "Deconstructed Chili" - Plain cooked beef chunks, rinsed beans, plain potatoes.
*   **Sunday (Dad's):** **One-Pot Braised Chicken Thighs with Carrots & Potatoes.** Uses **Chicken Thighs** and **Carrots**. Easy to cook in a dutch oven, travels perfectly, and leaves great leftovers.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Plain roasted chicken thigh and carrot sticks.
*   **Monday:** **Pan-Seared Cod with Warm Kale & White Bean Salad.** Uses **Cod Filets** and **Garden Kale**.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Plain seared fish with white rice.
*   **Tuesday:** **Steak & Roasted Vegetable Platter.** **Ribeye Steaks** served with a massive tray of roasted Brussels sprouts (crispy!) and zucchini (instead of mushrooms).
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Steak strips and raw veggie sticks with dip.
*   **Wednesday:** **Ground Chicken "Egg Roll in a Bowl".** Uses **Ground Chicken**. A stir-fry of cabbage, carrots, and onions with ginger/garlic.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* "Chicken & Rice" - Ground chicken mixed with rice, no sauce.

**Key New Ingredients:** Bell Peppers, Kidney/Black Beans, Cabbage, Brussels Sprouts, Zucchini.

## Option 2: The "Comfort Classics" üç≤
*Warm, familiar flavors adapted for your health goals.*

*   **Saturday (Tonight):** **White Bean Chicken Chili.** Uses **Ground Chicken** and **Pork Broth**. Creamy (blended beans) but dairy-free base, topped with avocado.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Chicken and white bean "soup" with cheese toast.
*   **Sunday (Dad's):** **Classic Pot Roast (Stew style).** Uses **Chuck Stew Meat**. Slow cooked with tons of carrots, celery, and onions. Served over **Cauliflower Mash** (to boost veg).
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Beef chunks and plain mashed potatoes.
*   **Monday:** **Sheet Pan Pollock Puttanesca.** Uses **Pollock Filets** and **Canned Tomatoes**. Roasted with capers (salt hit!) and olives (swapped for artichoke hearts if texture is an issue, or kept large to pick out).
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Plain fish and pasta.
*   **Tuesday:** **Lemon-Herb Roasted Chicken Thighs.** Uses **Chicken Thighs** and **Garden Herbs**. Served with a quinoa salad (need to buy quinoa).
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Chicken drumstick (if available) or thigh with quinoa.
*   **Wednesday:** **Steak Fajita Bowls.** **Ribeye Steaks** served over a bed of roasted peppers and onions with a side of black beans.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Steak and cheese quesadilla (corn tortilla).

**Key New Ingredients:** Cannellini Beans, Poblano Peppers, Cauliflower, Artichoke Hearts (optional), Quinoa, Bell Peppers.

## Option 3: The "Tex-Mex Twist" üåÆ
*Bold flavors and leveraging your masa/tortilla interest.*

*   **Saturday (Tonight):** **Texas-Style Red Chili (No Beans).** Uses **Chuck Stew Meat** and dried chiles (buy). Served with a massive **Avocado & Kale Salad** to hit the veg count.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Beef chunks in a soft corn taco.
*   **Sunday (Dad's):** **Chicken Tinga Tostadas.** Uses **Chicken Thighs** and **Chipotle in Adobo**. You bring the meat and tostada shells; assemble there. Easy leftovers.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Plain chicken tostada with cheese.
*   **Monday:** **Baja Fish Bowls.** Uses **Pollock Filets** (battered & pan-fried). Served over cabbage slaw with a lime vinaigrette.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Fish sticks (homemade) and corn.
*   **Tuesday:** **Steak & Sweet Potato Hash.** **Ribeye Steaks** served with roasted sweet potato cubes (cooled) and kale.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Steak and sweet potato cubes.
*   **Wednesday:** **Chicken Albondigas Soup (Meatball Soup).** Uses **Ground Chicken**. Light broth with zucchini, carrots, and potatoes.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Chicken meatballs and broth with rice.

**Key New Ingredients:** Dried Chiles (Ancho/Guajillo), Avocado, Cabbage, Sweet Potatoes, Zucchini, Chipotles in Adobo.

## Option 4: The "Garden & Grain" ü•ó
*Focuses on your garden kale and pantry grains.*

*   **Saturday (Tonight):** **Beef & Quinoa Chili.** Uses **Ground Chicken** (swapped for lighter) or **Chuck Meat**. Quinoa is cooked *in* the chili for texture and fiber.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Chili mixed with extra rice/cheese.
*   **Sunday (Dad's):** **Roasted Chicken Thighs with Sourdough Panzanella.** Uses **Chicken Thighs**. The "salad" is roasted peppers, tomatoes, onions, and toasted sourdough chunks (eats like a meal).
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Chicken and plain bread chunks.
*   **Monday:** **Seared Cod with Lentils.** Uses **Cod Filets**. Lentils (buy) are a great slow carb.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Fish and plain lentils/rice.
*   **Tuesday:** **Steak with Chimichurri & Roasted Root Veg.** **Ribeye Steaks** with garden herb chimichurri. Roasted carrots and parsnips.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Steak and carrot "fries".
*   **Wednesday:** **Sausage & Kale Soup (Zuppa Toscana style).** Uses **Smoked Sausages** and **Garden Kale**. Creamy broth (coconut milk or splash of cream) with potatoes.
    *   *Kid Submeal:* Sausage slices and potatoes.

**Key New Ingredients:** Quinoa, Bell Peppers, Lentils, Parsnips, Coconut Milk/Cream.
