## üë®‚Äçüç≥ Provisional Recipe: Coconut Chicken & Potato "Chowder"

### Ingredients (Refined 1:5:5:5)
*   **Protein:** 4-5 Chicken Breasts
*   **Starch:** ~1.5 lbs Red Potatoes, diced
*   **Fat:** 1 can Coconut Milk, 2 tbsp Butter, 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt
*   **Fiber/Veg:** 1/2 Head Cabbage (shredded), 2 cups Fresh Spinach, 1 Yellow Onion (diced), 2 Bell Peppers (diced)
*   **Flavor:** 2 tbsp Minced Garlic, 1 tbsp Bouillon, 1 tsp Aleppo Pepper, 1/4 cup Parsley, 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar.
*   **Seeded Crunch:** 1 tbsp Crushed Flax, 1 tsp Nigella, 1 tbsp Sesame seeds.

### Instructions
1. **Saut√©:** Set IP to Saut√©. Add **Coconut Oil**, diced **Onion**, and **Bell Peppers**. Cook until soft (~5 mins). Add **Garlic** and **Aleppo Pepper** for the last minute.
2. **Pressure Cook:** Add diced **Potatoes**, **Chicken Breasts**, **Bouillon**, and **Coconut Milk**. (Add a splash of water if needed to reach the minimum liquid line).
3. **Timer:** Manual **High Pressure for 10 minutes**.
4. **Shred & Melt:** Quick release. Remove chicken and shred with forks. While chicken is out, mash a few potato chunks into the broth. Stir in **Butter**, **Greek Yogurt**, and **Cottage Cheese**.
5. **Wilt:** Stir the shredded chicken back in. Add the thinly shredded **Cabbage** and **Fresh Spinach**. Close the lid (non-sealing) for 3-5 minutes to let the residual heat wilt the greens.
6. **Finish:** Stir in **Apple Cider Vinegar** and **Fresh Parsley**.
7. **Serve:** Top each bowl with the **Crushed Flax, Sesame, and Nigella seeds**. Grate **Parmesan** over the top.
