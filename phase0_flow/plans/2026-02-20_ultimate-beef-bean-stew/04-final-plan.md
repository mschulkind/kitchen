## 🥘 Recipe Summary: Ultimate Beef & Bean Stew

### 1. Mise en Place (12 min)
*   **Veggie Prep:** Dice 1-2 onions, 2-3 bell peppers, 2-3 carrots, and 2-3 stalks of celery. Mince 3-4 cloves of garlic.
*   **Tomato Prep:** Open the 28oz can of whole peeled tomatoes. Hand-crush them directly into a bowl (or the pot later) for a rustic, chunky texture.
*   **Bean Prep:** Open both cans of refried black beans (Pure Fat-Free and Amy's).

### 2. Sear & Sauté (15 min)
*   **Beef:** In a large Dutch oven or pot, brown the **2 lbs of ground beef** over high heat. Drain excess fat if needed, but leave a bit for flavor!
*   **Aromatics:** Add onions, bell peppers, carrots, and celery. Sauté until softened (about 8-10 mins). Stir in garlic and cook for 1 minute more.

### 3. The "Ultimate" Meld (15 min)
*   **Liquids:** Stir in the hand-crushed tomatoes, both cans of refried beans, and 1-2 tsp of beef bouillon.
*   **Spices:** Add 2 tbsp chili powder, 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tsp Aleppo pepper (or red pepper flakes), and 1 tsp dried oregano. Salt and pepper to taste.
*   **Simmer:** Reduce heat to medium-low and let it simmer for 15 minutes. The refried beans will "melt" into the tomato juice, creating a thick, velvety gravy.

### 4. The Finish (3 min)
*   **Avocado:** Slice or dice your **avocado**.
*   **Serve:** Ladle into bowls. Top with a massive dollop of **Greek yogurt** (sour cream sub), **shredded cheese**, and the **avocado**.
*   **Garnish:** A sprinkle of parmesan or extra red pepper flakes if you're feeling adventurous!
