### Option 1 (formerly 8): üêî Chicken & Masa Dumplings (Chochoyotes)

This is a classic Mexican comfort dish. We'll make a rich chicken and vegetable soup and then drop in "chochoyotes" - tender little dumplings made from the same masa harina you use for tortillas. They're dense, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying. We can even knead fresh herbs from your garden right into the dough.

*   **Theme**: Authentic Mexican comfort food.
*   **Key Ingredients**: `Chicken Broth`, `Cubed Cooked Chicken`, `Masa Harina`, `Purple Potatoes`, `Poblano Peppers`.
*   **Inspiration**:
    *   [Recipe: Chochoyotes, Mexican Corn Masa Dumplings - The Daring Gourmet](https://www.daringgourmet.com/chochoyotes-mexican-corn-masa-dumplings/)
    *   [Recipe: Caldo de Pollo con Bolitas de Masa (Chicken Soup with Masa Dumplings) - Pati Jinich](https://patijinich.com/caldo-de-pollo-con-bolitas-de-masa/)

#### üëâ Your Choice: How hearty do you want it?

*   **A) Brothy & Light:** We'll focus on a flavorful, clear chicken broth base, letting the individual components shine. The chochoyotes will be small and delicate, swimming with chunks of chicken and purple potato.
*   **B) Thick & Hearty Stew:** We'll create a thicker, more rustic stew. The chochoyotes can be made a bit larger, and we can wilt in some of your garden `Swiss Chard` for a nutritional boost, making it a true one-pot meal.
