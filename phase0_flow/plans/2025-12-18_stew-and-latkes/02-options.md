# Meal Plan Options

Here are the options for Thursday and Saturday separated out. You can mix and match any Thursday stew with any Saturday dinner.

## Thursday Options: The Stew Variations

*All Thursday options are designed to provide leftovers for Friday.*

### 1. The Original Maghrebi Stew

- **Concept:** The exact recipe requested. Rich tomato & lentil base, cumin/coriander/cinnamon, fig spread, lemon, topped with marinated feta and dill.
- **Vibe:** Deep, earthy, sweet-and-savory.
- **Shopping List:** Sourdough Loaf, Brown Lentils, Fig Spread, Feta Block, Fresh Dill, Lemons.

### 2. Smoky Red Pepper & Chickpea Stew

- **Concept:** A variation adding roasted red peppers (blended or chopped) and smoked paprika. Swaps half the lentils for chickpeas.
- **Vibe:** Sweet, smoky, and very mild/approachable for kids.
- **Shopping List:** Sourdough Loaf, Jarred Roasted Red Peppers, Chickpeas (canned or dry), Smoked Paprika.

### 3. "Green" Lentil & Spinach Stew

- **Concept:** Reduces the tomato paste, folds in lots of fresh spinach/chard at the end. Topped with a Yogurt-Dill swirl.
- **Vibe:** Fresh, herbaceous, and lighter.
- **Shopping List:** Sourdough Loaf, Spinach or Swiss Chard (large bag), Greek Yogurt, Fresh Dill.

### 4. Winter Root Vegetable Stew

- **Concept:** Adds cubes of roasted butternut squash or sweet potato to the lentil base. Uses sage and thyme. Topped with Goat Cheese.
- **Vibe:** Sweet, autumnal, and comforting.
- **Shopping List:** Sourdough Loaf, Butternut Squash, Goat Cheese Log, Fresh Sage.

### 5. "Harira-Style" Stew

- **Concept:** Inspired by Moroccan Harira. Turmeric, ginger, celery, lentils + chickpeas. Finished with cilantro and lemon.
- **Vibe:** Zesty, aromatic, and complex.
- **Shopping List:** Sourdough Loaf, Celery, Fresh Ginger, Chickpeas, Fresh Cilantro, Lemons.

## Saturday Options: The Dinner Party

*Designed for 4 adults + 5 kids. All options include your Latkes as a key side.*

### A. Slow-Cooked Beef Brisket

- **Concept:** Melt-in-your-mouth brisket with onions and carrots. Savory gravy for dipping latkes. Served with Matzo Ball Soup.
- **Vibe:** The quintessential Jewish holiday meal.
- **Shopping List:** Beef Brisket (4-5 lbs), Carrots, Parsnips, Matzo Meal, Eggs, Fresh Dill (for soup).

### B. Chicken Schnitzel & Israeli Salad

- **Concept:** Thin, crispy breaded chicken cutlets. Served with a fresh chopped salad of tomato/cucumber.
- **Vibe:** Kid-favorite fried feast.
- **Shopping List:** Chicken Breasts (3 lbs), Panko/Breadcrumbs, Cucumbers, Tomatoes, Bell Peppers, Lemons.

### C. Roast Chicken with Apples

- **Concept:** Whole roasted chicken with apples, onions, and thyme. Served with Roasted Green Beans and Hummus/Pita.
- **Vibe:** Elegant, lighter protein to balance the latkes.
- **Shopping List:** Whole Chickens (2), Apples (Gala/Granny Smith), Green Beans, Hummus, Pita Bread.

### D. Roasted Salmon & "Latke Bar"

- **Concept:** Roasted Salmon side with lemon/dill. Plus a "Bar" with smoked salmon, creme fraiche, chives. Served with Arugula Salad.
- **Vibe:** "Appetizing" style, fancy but low-effort.
- **Shopping List:** Salmon Side (3 lbs), Smoked Salmon, Creme Fraiche, Chives, Arugula, Pears.

### E. Sweet & Sour Meatballs

- **Concept:** Beef meatballs simmered in a traditional sweet & sour tomato sauce (cranberry/apricot base). Served with Steamed Broccoli.
- **Vibe:** Classic party dish, sweet and saucy (kids love it).
- **Shopping List:** Ground Beef (3 lbs), Cranberry Sauce or Apricot Jam, Broccoli Crowns.

## üí° Recommended Pairings (Maximum Synergy)

If you want to minimize your shopping list and save money, choose one of these "Power Pairs":

### üèÜ The "Dill & Lemon" Bundle (Option 1 + A or D)

- **Why:** Both the **Original Maghrebi Stew** and the **Brisket (A)** or **Salmon (D)** rely heavily on fresh Dill and Lemons.
- **Savings:** Buy one large "bulk" bunch of dill and a 2lb bag of lemons. You'll use them across every single meal.
- **Best for:** Fans of bright, acidic, and herbaceous flavors.

### üèÜ The "Creamy & Fresh" Bundle (Option 3 + D)

- **Why:** The **Green Lentil Stew** uses Yogurt, and the **Salmon Bar (D)** uses Creme Fraiche.
- **Savings:** Buy one large tub of high-quality Greek Yogurt. It works perfectly as the stew swirl AND as the creamy topping for latkes/salmon.
- **Best for:** A lighter, more modern feeling menu.

### üèÜ The "Produce Power" Bundle (Option 5 + B)

- **Why:** The **Harira Stew** and **Schnitzel/Israeli Salad (B)** both require a lot of "crisper drawer" items (Lemons, Celery, Cucumbers, Tomatoes).
- **Savings:** You can buy your produce in one go and use the "scraps" (like celery hearts or extra parsley) across both nights.
- **Best for:** If you want a very fresh, vegetable-forward week.

### üèÜ The "Pantry Hero" Bundle (Option 2 + E)

- **Why:** The **Smoky Red Pepper Stew** and **Sweet & Sour Meatballs** rely mostly on jarred/canned goods (Roasted Peppers, Chickpeas, Cranberry Sauce).
- **Savings:** Lowest "fresh" grocery bill. Most ingredients are shelf-stable or frozen (like the ground beef).
- **Best for:** A busy week where you don't want to worry about produce wilting.

## Ingredient Synergies & Analysis

### üåø Fresh Herbs

- **Dill:** Strong synergy. If you choose **Thursday #1 or #3** AND **Saturday A or D**, buy a huge bunch of dill.
- **Cilantro:** Only used in **Thursday #5**.
- **Sage/Thyme:** **Thursday #4** uses Sage. **Saturday C** uses Thyme.

### üçã Citrus (Lemons)

- **High Synergy:** Lemons are essential for **Thursday #1 & #5** and **Saturday B, C, & D**. If you pick any combination of these, buy a bag of lemons.

### ü•ï Root Vegetables

- **Carrots:** You have some in the fridge, but **Saturday A (Brisket)** and **Saturday A (Soup)** will require a fresh bag.
- **Celery:** Only needed for **Thursday #5** or **Saturday A (Soup)**.

  - *Tip:* If you choose Thursday #3 and Saturday D, you could potentially use Greek Yogurt for both the stew swirl and the latke topping to save buying two different creamy dairy items.
