## Recipe: Creamy Tomato & "Ricotta" Chicken Stew ğŸ…ğŸ¥£

### Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs Chicken Breast (parfrozen and sliced thin)
- 1 cup Quinoa (rinsed)
- 1 can (28oz) Whole Peeled Tomatoes
- 1 cup Cottage Cheese (to be blended)
- 3-4 cups Beef or Pork Broth (from freezer)
- 1 Onion, 2 Carrots, 2 Celery stalks, 2 Bell Peppers (all diced)
- 3 cloves Garlic (minced)
- 2 cups Mixed Greens or Frozen Spinach
- 1 tbsp Dried Basil, 1 tsp Dried Oregano
- Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper, Red Pepper Flakes
- **Garnish:** Sliced radishes, Shredded parmesan

### Instructions
1. **SautÃ©:** Soften onion, carrots, celery, and peppers in olive oil. Add garlic and dried herbs for 1 minute.
2. **Simmer:** Add hand-crushed tomatoes, broth, and quinoa. Bring to boil, simmer covered for 10 mins.
3. **Poach:** Stir in sliced chicken; cook for 5-7 mins until opaque.
4. **Creamify:** Blend cottage cheese with a ladle of hot broth until smooth; stir back into the pot.
5. **Wilt:** Stir in greens until just wilted.
6. **Serve:** Garnish with radishes for crunch and parmesan if desired.
