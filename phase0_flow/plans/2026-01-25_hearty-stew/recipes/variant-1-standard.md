# Creamy Tomato & "Ricotta" Chicken Stew üçÖ

*A creamy, high-protein stew without the cream.*

### Ingredients & Prep

<div class="prep-container">
<div class="prep-group">
<span class="prep-category">Ready to Use (No Board Work)</span>

- **1 cup** Quinoa (rinsed)
- **1 can** Whole Peeled Tomatoes (28oz)
- **4 cups** Broth (Beef/Pork)
- **1 cup** Cottage Cheese
- **2 cups** Mixed Greens / Spinach
- **Spices:** Basil (1T), Oregano (1t)
- **Pantry:** Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper
</div>

<div class="prep-group">
<span class="prep-category">To Prep (Knife Work)</span>

- **1.5 lbs** Chicken Breast $\rightarrow$ *Slice thin*
- **1 large** Yellow Onion $\rightarrow$ *Dice*
- **2-3** Carrots $\rightarrow$ *Dice*
- **2 stalks** Celery $\rightarrow$ *Dice*
- **2** Bell Peppers $\rightarrow$ *Dice*
- **3 cloves** Garlic $\rightarrow$ *Mince*
- **Radishes** $\rightarrow$ *Slice thin*
- **Parmesan** $\rightarrow$ *Grate*
</div>
</div>
