# Creamy Tomato & "Ricotta" Chicken Stew üçÖü•£

A high-protein, veggie-packed stew that uses blended cottage cheese to mimic the richness of ricotta and cream without the heavy fat.

- **Prep time:** 15 mins
- **Cook time:** 25 mins
- **Total time:** 40 mins
- **Servings:** 4-6

## Ingredients

### The Base (Mirepoix & Veg)
- 1 tbsp Olive Oil
- 1 large Yellow Onion, diced
- 2 Carrots, diced
- 2 Celery stalks, diced
- 2 Bell Peppers, chopped
- 3 cloves Garlic, minced

### The Stew Body
- 1.5 lbs Chicken Breast, sliced thin (parfrozen for easier cutting)
- 1 can (28oz) Whole Peeled Tomatoes (crush by hand or with a spoon in the pot)
- 1 cup Quinoa, rinsed
- 3-4 cups Broth (Pork or Beef broth from freezer works well here)
- 1 tbsp Dried Basil
- 1 tsp Dried Oregano
- 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
- Salt and Pepper to taste

### The Creamy Element
- 1 cup Cottage Cheese

### The Finish
- 2 cups Mixed Greens or Frozen Spinach
- Sliced Radishes (for garnish)
- Shredded Parmesan (optional)

---

## Instructions

### 1. Mise-en-place
- Saut√© the **onion, carrots, celery, and peppers** in olive oil over medium-high heat in a large pot until softened (5-7 mins).
- Add the **garlic** and spices (basil, oregano, pepper flakes) and cook for 1 min until fragrant.

### 2. The Simmer
- Pour in the **canned tomatoes** (crushing them as you go) and the **broth**.
- Stir in the **rinsed quinoa**. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
- Cover and cook for 10 minutes.

### 3. The Protein
- Stir in the **sliced chicken**. Because it's sliced thin, it will poach in the hot liquid in about 5-7 minutes.

### 4. The Creamy Transformation
- While the chicken cooks, place the **cottage cheese** in a blender (or use an immersion blender) with a splash of the hot stew liquid. Blend until completely smooth.
- Stir the blended cottage cheese back into the pot. It will turn the red sauce into a creamy, pinkish "vodka sauce" style consistency.

### 5. Final Touch
- Stir in the **mixed greens** or **frozen spinach** and cook until wilted (1-2 mins).
- Taste and adjust salt and pepper.
