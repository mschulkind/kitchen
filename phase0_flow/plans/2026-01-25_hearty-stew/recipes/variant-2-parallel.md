*A creamy, high-protein stew without the cream.*

### Mise-en-place
*Get these ready before you start the timer.*
- **Produce:** Dice Onion, Carrots, Celery, Bell Peppers. Minced Garlic.
- **Chicken:** Slice par-frozen chicken into thin strips.
- **Pantry:** Rinse Quinoa. Open Cans.
