# Creamy Tomato & "Ricotta" Chicken Stew (Variant 3: High Density) üçÖ

*A creamy, high-protein stew without the cream.*

### Mise-en-place
- **Dice:** Onion, Carrots, Celery, Peppers.
- **Slice:** Chicken (thin strips).
- **Rinse:** Quinoa.

---

### Execution Steps

| Time | Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **T-40** | **SAUT√â** | **1 tbsp Oil** + **Mirepoix**. Med-High. Soften veg (7m). |
| **T-33** | **BLOOM** | **3 cloves Garlic** + **Herbs** (Basil/Oregano). 1 min. |
| **T-32** | **BOIL** | **28oz Tomatoes** + **4c Broth** + **1c Quinoa**. Crush tomatoes. |
| **T-30** | **SIMMER** | Cover and cook (10m). |
| **T-20** | **POACH** | **1.5lbs Chicken**. Stir in. Uncovered (5-7m). |
| **T-15** | **BLEND** | **1c Cottage Cheese** + **Ladle of Broth**. Blend smooth. |
| **T-10** | **COMBINE** | Pour cheese mix into pot. Stir well. Low heat. |
| **T-05** | **WILT** | **2c Greens**. Stir in. Season S&P. |
| **T-00** | **EAT** | Top with **Radish** & **Parmesan**. |
