# Plan Options: STK Sirloin Steak & Cauliflower Night

**Date:** Wednesday, March 4, 2026
**Goal:** A high-quality STK meal using existing stock.

---

## 📅 The Options

| Option | Title | Main Components & "Steer" | Kid-Friendly Path |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Aleppo-Garlic Steak & Roasted Cauliflower "Rice" Bowl** | **Steak:** Sliced thin, seared fast with Aleppo/garlic. **Veg:** Cauliflower "rice" or fine florets and roasted with peppers. **Sauce:** A cool Greek yogurt/lemon/tahini drizzle. | Serve steak strips plain with buttered pasta or rice. |
| **2** | **Sheet Pan Steak Fajitas with Cauliflower "Fries"** | **Steak:** Rubbed with cumin/paprika, broiled with peppers and onions. **Veg:** Cauliflower cut into planks/wedges and roasted until crispy. **Finish:** Lime juice and optional cheese. | Steak and peppers in a tortilla or plain with cheese. |
| **3** | **Creamy Beef & Cauliflower Skillet (Low-GI)** | **Steak:** Cross-cut strips seared. **Veg:** Peppers and bite-sized cauliflower simmered in a light bouillon/garlic broth. **Finish:** Folded with cottage cheese or Greek yogurt for a velvety finish. | Set aside seared steak strips and plain cauliflower before saucing. |
| **4** | **Steak & Cauliflower Pepper Stir-Fry** | **Steak:** Marinated in soy/ginger (if you have it) or just salt/pepper/garlic. **Veg:** Peppers and cauliflower florets stir-fry high-heat. **Texture:** Topped with sesame seeds or crushed flax for crunch. | Steak strips and peppers over plain noodles/rice. |
| **5** | **Beef & Pepper Cauliflower "Hash"** | **Steak:** Small diced bits. **Veg:** Finely chopped cauliflower and peppers sautéed together until browned and tender (like a potato hash replacement). **Finish:** Topped with a fried egg or feta. | Separate the steak bits and larger cauliflower florets. |
