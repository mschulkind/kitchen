## 🥘 Recipe Summary: Creamy Sesame-Hemp Skillet

### 1. Mise en Place (10 min)
*   **Veg:** Dice onion and peppers. Chop cauliflower into small, bite-sized florets.
*   **Steak:** Thinly slice the partially frozen steak *across the grain* into short strips.
*   **Sesame:** Toast 3 tbsp sesame seeds in a dry skillet, then pulse in the **coffee grinder** until they form a coarse powder.

### 2. Hard Sear (5 min)
*   Sear steak strips in a smoking hot skillet with butter and oil until browned (1-2 mins per side).
*   **PULL:** Set aside a portion of plain steak strips for the kids.
*   Season adult portion with half the **Aleppo pepper**.

### 3. Sauté & Steam (15 min)
*   Sauté onion and peppers in the same pan. Add cauliflower, garlic, and cumin.
*   Deglaze with bouillon or water, cover, and steam until cauliflower is tender-crisp.
*   **PULL:** Set aside a portion of plain cauliflower for the kids.

### 4. The Creamy Finish (5 min)
*   Lower heat. Return steak to the pan.
*   Fold in **Greek yogurt**, the **ground sesame powder**, **Hemp hearts**, and **Nigella seeds**.
*   Stir until a velvety sauce coats everything. Finish with lime juice.

### 5. Garnish & Serve (2 min)
*   Serve in warm bowls.
*   Top with a heavy hit of the **Freezer Seed Mix** and remaining Aleppo pepper.
