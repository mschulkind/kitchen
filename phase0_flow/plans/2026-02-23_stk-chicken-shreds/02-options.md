# Meal Options: The "Smushed" Chicken Riff 🥘

**Theme:** 5 Hybrids of Warm Salad & Egg Roll Bowl
**Focus:** 7-hour braised smushed chicken thighs + shaved sprouts/cabbage (No Fresh Ginger).

---

### Option A: Garlic-Balsamic Shaved Sprout Skillet 🥗🧄
*   **Vibe:** Heavy on the garlic and balsamic. Shaved brussels sprouts sautéed with plenty of minced garlic and a pinch of **ginger powder**. The smushed chicken gets slightly crispy, then deglazed with balsamic and chicken stock.
*   **Crunch:** Topped with toasted sesame seeds and pumpkin seeds.

### Option B: "Savory-Soy" Smushed Chicken Slaw 🥢🥡
*   **The Base:** Shaved sprouts/cabbage and carrots stir-fried with garlic and ginger powder. The smushed chicken is folded in to coat everything like a rich sauce.

**5 Ways to Take It:**

1.  **The "Deep Umami" Route:** Double down on the bouillon and balsamic. Let it cook down until the glaze is thick, dark, and sticky. 
2.  **The "Zesty & Bright" Route:** Add a heavy squeeze of lemon and some extra vinegar at the very end to create a "hot-and-sour" vibe that cuts the chicken's richness.
3.  **The "Spicy & Bold" Route:** Add a generous amount of Aleppo pepper and a pinch of cayenne. The heat will play beautifully with the fatty braised chicken.
4.  **The "Creamy Slaw" Route:** After removing from heat, stir in a dollop of Greek yogurt. This turns the glaze into a rich, creamy sauce that binds the smushed chicken to the veggies.
5.  **The "Nutty & Earthy" Route:** Toast the sesame and pumpkin seeds in the pan first, then stir them back in at the end. Use a touch more ginger powder for an earthier flavor.
