# Recipe: Spicy-Earthy Smushed Chicken & Sprout Slaw 🥗🔥

*   **Prep Time:** 10 mins
*   **Cook Time:** 15 mins
*   **Total Time:** 25 mins
*   **Theme:** STK / Low-GI / Hybrid Slaw

## Ingredients

### Produce
*   1 small bag Brussels Sprouts, finely shaved
*   1 Carrot, grated
*   1/4 Yellow Onion, thinly sliced
*   2 cloves Garlic, minced

### Protein
*   2 cups Smushed Braised Chicken Thighs (7-hour braise)

### Pantry / Spices
*   1 tbsp Olive Oil or Avocado Oil
*   1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
*   1/2 tsp Chicken Bouillon
*   1/2 tsp Ginger Powder
*   1/2 tsp Aleppo Pepper
*   1 pinch Cayenne Pepper (or more for heat)
*   1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
*   1 tbsp Pumpkin Seeds
*   Salt and Black Pepper, to taste

---

## Instructions

### 1. Mise en Place
Finely shave the brussels sprouts using a mandoline or sharp knife. Grate the carrot. Mince the garlic and thinly slice the onion. Ensure your chicken is "smushed" and ready to go.

### 2. Toast Seeds
In a large skillet over medium heat, toast the sesame and pumpkin seeds until fragrant and golden (2-3 mins). Remove and set aside.

### 3. Sauté Veg
Wipe out the pan and add the oil. Increase heat to medium-high. Sauté the onion for 2 minutes, then toss in the shaved sprouts and grated carrot. Cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sprouts are slightly wilted and have some charred edges.

### 4. The Smush & Glaze
Stir in the garlic, ginger powder, aleppo pepper, and cayenne. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the smushed chicken, balsamic vinegar, and bouillon. Toss everything vigorously so the chicken coats the vegetables. Cook for 3-4 minutes until piping hot and the glaze is sticky.

### 5. Finish & Serve
Season with salt and pepper. Fold in half of the toasted seeds. Serve in bowls topped with the remaining seeds and an extra pinch of Aleppo pepper. 🥳✨
