## 🥗 Recipe Summary: Spicy-Earthy Smushed Chicken & Sprout Slaw

### 1. Mise en Place (10 min)
*   **Shave:** Finely shave the brussels sprouts (use a mandoline if you have one).
*   **Grate/Mince:** Grate 1 carrot and mince 2 cloves of garlic. Thinly slice 1/4 of an onion.
*   **Ready:** Get your smushed chicken, balsamic, bouillon, and spices ready.

### 2. Toast & Sauté (8 min)
*   **Seeds:** Toast 1 tbsp each of sesame and pumpkin seeds in a dry skillet until fragrant. Remove and set aside.
*   **Veg:** Heat 1 tbsp oil in the pan. Sauté the onion for 2 mins, then add the shaved sprouts and carrot. Cook until slightly wilted and charred in spots.

### 3. The Smush & Glaze (7 min)
*   **Aromatics:** Stir in the garlic, 1/2 tsp ginger powder, 1/2 tsp Aleppo pepper, and a pinch of cayenne for 1 minute.
*   **Finish:** Add the smushed chicken, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, and 1/2 tsp bouillon. Toss vigorously for 3-4 minutes until the chicken coats the veggies and the glaze is sticky.
*   **Serve:** Fold in half the toasted seeds. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with the remaining seeds and a final sprinkle of Aleppo pepper.
