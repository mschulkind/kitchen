## 🥘 Recipe Summary: Golden Curry Coconut Roots & Greens

### 1. Mise en Place (10 min)
*   **Aromatics:** Dice 1 yellow onion and mince 3 cloves of garlic.
*   **Protein:** Shred or chop your leftover braised chicken thighs.
*   **Spices:** Get your cumin, coriander, and ginger powder ready.
*   **Liquid:** Open the 13.5 oz can of coconut milk and measure 1 cup of your braised chicken stock.

### 2. Sauté & Bloom (10 min)
*   **Schmaltz:** Melt 1 tbsp of the chicken schmaltz (fat from the stock) in a large pot over medium heat.
*   **Aromatics:** Sauté the onion until translucent (5 mins), then add the garlic for 1 minute.
*   **Spices:** Stir in 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander, and 1/2 tsp ginger powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

### 3. Simmer (7 min)
*   **Combine:** Pour in the coconut milk and 1 cup of chicken stock.
*   **Bulk:** Add the frozen fire-roasted root vegetables and the shredded chicken.
*   **Meld:** Bring to a gentle simmer for 5-7 minutes until the veggies are hot and the flavors have melded.

### 4. The Finish (3 min)
*   **Greens:** Stir in 2-3 cups of fresh spinach until wilted.
*   **Season:** Add salt and black pepper to taste.
*   **Garnish:** Serve in bowls topped with a sprinkle of Aleppo pepper.
