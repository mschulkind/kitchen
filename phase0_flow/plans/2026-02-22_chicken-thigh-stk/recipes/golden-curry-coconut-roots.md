# Recipe: Golden Curry Coconut Roots & Greens 🥘

A creamy, aromatic stew that uses leftover braised chicken and fire-roasted root vegetables for a fast, Low-GI meal.

- **Total Time:** 30 Minutes
- **Servings:** 2-4
- **Dietary:** Low-GI, Dairy-Free, STK

## Ingredients

### Prep / Mise en Place
- **1** Yellow Onion, diced
- **3 cloves** Garlic, minced
- **1-2 cups** Braised Chicken Thighs, shredded or chopped
- **1 tbsp** Chicken Schmaltz (the fat from your braise liquid)

### The Sauce & Body
- **13.5 oz can** Coconut Milk
- **1 cup** Chicken Stock (from your braise)
- **1 bag** Fire-roasted Root Vegetables (Freezer)
- **2-3 cups** Fresh Spinach

### Spices
- **1 tsp** Ground Cumin
- **1 tsp** Ground Coriander
- **1/2 tsp** Ginger Powder
- **1/2 tsp** Aleppo Pepper (for garnish)
- **Salt & Black Pepper** to taste

---

## Instructions

1.  **Sauté Aromatics:** In a large pot, melt the **chicken schmaltz** over medium heat. Add the **onion** and sauté until soft and translucent (5-6 mins). Stir in the **garlic** for 1 minute.
2.  **Bloom Spices:** Add the **cumin, coriander, and ginger powder**. Stir for 1-2 minutes until you can really smell them.
3.  **Simmer:** Pour in the **coconut milk** and **chicken stock**. Add the **frozen root vegetables** and the **chicken**. Simmer gently for 7-10 minutes.
4.  **Wilt Greens:** Stir in the **fresh spinach**. Once it wilts (about 2 mins), it's done!
5.  **Finish:** Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with **Aleppo pepper** before serving.

---

## 🧒 Kid Pull
Before adding the spinach (or even the spices if they are very picky), pull some plain chicken and root veggies into a separate bowl.
