# Options: Braised Chicken Thigh STK 🍗

We're diving into the "ton" of braised chicken thighs you have! Keeping things **Low-GI** and **Shop the Kitchen** (STK) friendly. No shopping required! 🏠✨

---

## 🥥 Coconut Milk Options (3/5)

### Option 1: Golden Coconut Chicken & Chickpea Stew 🥘
*   **The Vibe:** A thick, aromatic stew that feels like a hug.
*   **Key Ingredients:** Braised chicken thighs, **coconut milk**, canned chickpeas, fresh spinach, onions, and a blend of cumin, coriander, and Aleppo pepper.
*   **Low-GI Win:** Chickpeas and spinach provide fiber and protein to keep the GI low.
*   **Kid Pull:** Pull some plain chicken and chickpeas before the spices and coconut milk go in.

### Option 2: Zesty Thai-Style Coconut Chicken Soup 🥣
*   **The Vibe:** Bright, creamy, and slightly spicy.
*   **Key Ingredients:** Braised chicken thighs, **coconut milk**, bell peppers, carrots, ginger powder, garlic, and a heavy squeeze of lemon.
*   **Low-GI Win:** Loaded with non-starchy veggies (peppers, carrots, spinach).
*   **Kid Pull:** Save some chicken and carrots to serve on the side.

### Option 3: Creamy Coconut Roots & Greens 🌿
*   **The Vibe:** Rustic and earthy, using those freezer gems.
*   **Key Ingredients:** Braised chicken thighs simmered in **coconut milk** with **fire-roasted root vegetables** (from the freezer) and a mountain of fresh spinach stirred in at the end.
*   **Low-GI Win:** Root veggies are balanced by the healthy fats in coconut milk and high-fiber spinach.

---

## 🥗 Non-Coconut Options (2/5)

### Option 4: Mediterranean Chicken & Chickpea Skillet 🍋
*   **The Vibe:** Bright, garlicky, and fresh.
*   **Key Ingredients:** Braised chicken thighs, chickpeas, garlic, lemon, and fresh spinach. Topped with sliced radishes for crunch and a dollop of Greek yogurt.
*   **Low-GI Win:** High protein, high fiber, zero refined carbs.

### Option 5: Homemade Chicken & Bean Tostadas 🌮
*   **The Vibe:** A fun, hands-on family dinner.
*   **Key Ingredients:** Fresh tortillas made from **Masa Harina**, topped with refried beans, braised chicken, avocado, radishes, and Greek yogurt (sour cream sub).
*   **Low-GI Win:** Masa harina (corn) is lower GI than flour, and the beans/avocado add tons of fiber and healthy fats.
