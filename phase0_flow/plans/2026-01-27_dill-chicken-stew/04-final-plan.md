## Recipe: Coconut Dill Chicken Stew ğŸ¥¥

### Ingredients
- **Protein:** 1.5 lbs Braised Chicken Thighs (Frozen)
- **Produce:** 1 Onion, 2-3 Carrots, 3 cloves Garlic, 1 inch Ginger (or powder)
- **Greens:** 2 cups Frozen Spinach, 1 bunch Fresh Dill
- **Pantry:** 1 can Coconut Milk, 1 cup Rice, Turmeric, Red Pepper Flakes, Oil, Vinegar/Lime
- **Liquid:** Splash of water or broth if needed

### Instructions
1.  **Rice:** Start rice in a separate pot (T-30).
2.  **SautÃ©:** Soften onion and carrots in oil (T-25).
3.  **Bloom:** Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, and chili flakes. Cook until fragrant (T-20).
4.  **Simmer:** Pour in coconut milk and add the braised chicken. Simmer gently for 10-15 mins to heat chicken through (T-18).
5.  **Finish:** Stir in frozen spinach (it wilts instantly) and the chopped fresh dill. Season with salt and a splash of vinegar or lime juice (T-5).
