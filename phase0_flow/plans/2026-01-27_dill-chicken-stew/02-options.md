**Title:** Coconut Dill Chicken Stew with Spinach & Rice
- **Concept:** A rich, comforting stew using coconut milk as the base. The sweetness of the coconut pairs surprisingly well with the grassy pop of fresh dill.
- **Key Ingredients:** Braised Chicken, Coconut Milk, Frozen Spinach, Onion, Garlic, Ginger (powder), Rice.

**Title:** Cinnamon-Tomato Chicken Stew with Dill & Orzo/Pasta
- **Concept:** Inspired by Greek *Kapama*, this uses canned tomatoes and warm spices (cinnamon/cloves) to create a deep red sauce, finished with a huge handful of fresh dill.
- **Key Ingredients:** Braised Chicken, Canned Tomatoes, Cinnamon stick (or powder), Pasta (or Rice), Onion.
- **Flavor Profile:** Savory, warming, sweet-spiced, acidic tomato.
- **Shopping List:** None.

**Title:** Turmeric & Chickpea Chicken Stew with Yoghurt-Dill Topping
- **Concept:** A golden broth base using turmeric and chickpeas for earthiness. The dill comes in at the end, mixed with Greek Yogurt for a cold/hot contrast topping.
- **Key Ingredients:** Braised Chicken, Canned Chickpeas, Turmeric, Potatoes, Mixed Greens, Greek Yogurt.
- **Flavor Profile:** Earthy, golden, brightened by the tangy yogurt-dill topping.
- **Shopping List:** None.
