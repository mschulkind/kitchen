Creamy Zest Spanish Chicken & Chickpeas 🍋

Ingredients & Prep

Ready to Use (No Board Work)
  • 1.5 lbs Chicken Breast
  • 15 oz can Chickpeas
  • 10 oz bag Frozen Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 tsp Aleppo Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Chicken Bouillon
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • to taste Salt and Pepper
  • Optional Parmesan Cheese
To Prep (Knife Work)
  • 1 Onion → diced
  • 2 Bell Peppers → diced
  • 2 stalks Celery → thinly sliced
  • 4 Radishes → thinly sliced into rounds
  • 4 cloves Garlic → minced

Execution Steps

Time Ingredients Action
T-30
⏱ 10 min
1.5 lbs Chicken Breast
1 Onion
2 Bell Peppers
2 stalks Celery
4 cloves Garlic
10 oz bag Frozen Spinach
Mise en Place: Cube chicken into bite-sized pieces. Dice onion and peppers. Slice celery and radishes. Mince garlic. Thaw spinach in microwave for 2 mins, then squeeze bone-dry in a kitchen towel. Zest and juice the lemon.
MEANWHILE
Spices & Yogurt → Mix yogurt with lemon zest and 1 tbsp of the lemon juice in a small bowl. Set aside.
T-20
⏱ 6 min
1 tbsp Olive Oil
1.5 lbs Chicken
Sear Chicken: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Sear until golden-brown on all sides (about 5-6 mins). It doesn't need to be fully cooked yet. Remove chicken to a plate.
T-14
⏱ 5 min
1 tbsp Olive Oil
1 Onion
2 Peppers
2 stalks Celery
4 cloves Garlic
Sauté Veggies: Add remaining oil to the same skillet. Sauté onion, peppers, and celery until they begin to soften (4 mins). Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 min until fragrant.
MEANWHILE
Chickpeas → Rinse and drain if not already done.
T-09
⏱ 6 min
15 oz can Chickpeas
1 tsp Aleppo Pepper
1/2 tsp Cumin
1/2 tsp Oregano
1 tsp Bouillon
10 oz bag Spinach
Spice & Simmer: Add chickpeas, all dry spices, and bouillon to the pan. Stir to coat. Return the chicken and any juices to the pan along with the squeezed spinach and a splash of water (2-3 tbsp). Cover and simmer for 5-6 mins until chicken is cooked through.
T-03
⏱ 3 min
1 tbsp Butter
1/2 cup Greek Yogurt Mixture
to taste Remaining Lemon Juice
Creamy Finish: REDUCE HEAT TO LOW. Stir in the butter until melted. Gently fold in the yogurt/lemon mixture. Stir constantly for 1-2 mins until a creamy sauce forms. Do not let it boil! Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, and more lemon juice if needed.
T-00
🍽️ Done!
4 Radishes
Optional Parmesan Cheese
Serve: Spoon into bowls. Garnish with sliced radishes for crunch and a dusting of Parmesan. Serve immediately.
MEANWHILE
Kids → PULL: If kids want plain components, save some seared chicken and chickpeas before the spice/yogurt steps.
