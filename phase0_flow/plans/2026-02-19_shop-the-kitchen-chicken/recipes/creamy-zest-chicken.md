# Creamy Zest Spanish Chicken & Chickpeas 🍋

**Time:** 30 minutes
**Macros:** 50% Veg / 25% Protein / 25% Starch (Low-GI)

---

## 🛒 Ingredients

### Protein & Pantry
- **1.5 lbs** Chicken Breast, cubed
- **15 oz can** Chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- **10 oz bag** Frozen Spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
- **1 tsp** Chicken Bouillon
- **2 tbsp** Olive Oil
- **1 tbsp** Butter

### Produce
- **1** Onion, diced
- **2** Bell Peppers, diced
- **2 stalks** Celery, thinly sliced
- **4** Radishes, thinly sliced
- **4 cloves** Garlic, minced
- **1** Lemon, zested and juiced

### Dairy & Spices
- **1/2 cup** Greek Yogurt
- **1 tsp** Aleppo Pepper
- **1/2 tsp** Cumin
- **1/2 tsp** Dried Oregano
- **To taste** Salt and Black Pepper
- **Optional** Parmesan Cheese

---

## 👨‍🍳 Instructions

### 1. Mise en Place
Cube the chicken. Dice the onion and peppers. Slice the celery and radishes. Mince the garlic. Zest and juice the lemon. Mix the yogurt with the lemon zest and 1 tbsp of the juice.

### 2. Sear Chicken
Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper and sear until browned on all sides (5-6 mins). Remove to a plate.

### 3. Sauté Aromatics
Add remaining oil. Sauté onion, peppers, and celery until softened (4 mins). Stir in garlic and cook 1 min.

### 4. Spice & Simmer
Add chickpeas, Aleppo pepper, cumin, oregano, bouillon, and squeezed spinach. Return chicken to the pan with a splash of water. Cover and simmer 5-6 mins until chicken is cooked through.

### 5. Creamy Finish
Reduce heat to low. Melt in the butter. Fold in the yogurt mixture and stir until creamy (1-2 mins). Do not boil.

### 6. Serve
Top with sliced radishes and Parmesan. Serve immediately.
