## 🥘 Recipe Summary: Creamy Zest Spanish Chicken

### 1. Mise en Place (10 min)
*   **Prep:** Cube chicken, dice onion/peppers, slice celery/radishes, and mince garlic.
*   **Spinach:** Thaw and squeeze the 10oz bag of frozen spinach until bone-dry.
*   **Sauce Base:** Mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with lemon zest and 1 tbsp juice.

### 2. Sear & Sauté (11 min)
*   **Chicken:** Sear 1.5 lbs cubed chicken in 1 tbsp oil until browned; set aside.
*   **Veggies:** Sauté onion, peppers, and celery in the same pan. Add garlic and spices (Aleppo, Cumin, Oregano) at the end.

### 3. Simmer (6 min)
*   **Build:** Return chicken to pan with 15oz can chickpeas, spinach, bouillon, and a splash of water.
*   **Cook:** Simmer covered until chicken is cooked through.

### 4. The Finish (3 min)
*   **Creamy:** Off the heat (or low heat), melt in 1 tbsp butter and fold in the yogurt mixture.
*   **Serve:** Garnish with radishes and Parmesan.
