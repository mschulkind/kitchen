# Options: The "Shop the Kitchen" One-Night Special (Low-GI / 50:25:25) 🥘

These options are designed to hit the **50% Veggie, 25% Protein, 25% Starch** ratio and maintain a **Low-GI focus** using your chickpeas and fibrous veggies.

---

## Option 1: Mediterranean Chicken & Chickpea Skillet 🍋
*   **Theme:** Bright & Zesty
*   **Composition:** 50% Veg (Spinach, Peppers, Onions), 25% Protein (Chicken), 25% Starch (Chickpeas).
*   **Low-GI Note:** Chickpeas provide slow-burning carbs and extra fiber.
*   **Description:** A fast one-pan wonder. Sautéed chicken chunks with crispy chickpeas, bell peppers, and fresh spinach. Finished with lemon, garlic, and cumin.

## Option 2: "Unstuffed" Cabbage & Chicken Bowls 🍲
*   **Theme:** Comforting & Hearty
*   **Composition:** 50% Veg (Cabbage, Carrots, Celery), 25% Protein (Chicken), 25% Starch (Chickpeas or small side of Quinoa).
*   **Low-GI Note:** Cabbage and Carrots provide high volume and fiber to slow glucose absorption.
*   **Description:** Shredded cabbage and carrots sautéed with chicken, onions, and celery. Finished with a creamy dill-yogurt sauce (healthy fats).

## Option 3: Quick Chicken & Cabbage Stir-Fry 🥢
*   **Theme:** Savory & Crunchy
*   **Composition:** 50% Veg (Cabbage, Carrots, Peppers, Radishes), 25% Protein (Chicken), 25% Starch (Quinoa or Chickpeas).
*   **Low-GI Note:** Swapping white rice for Quinoa or Chickpeas keeps it Low-GI.
*   **Description:** A high-heat stir-fry featuring chicken and a mountain of cabbage. Radishes are added at the end for crunch. 

## Option 4: Spanish-Style Chickpea & Chicken Sauté 🥘
*   **Theme:** Smoky & Warm
*   **Composition:** 50% Veg (Peppers, Onion, Spinach), 25% Protein (Chicken), 25% Starch (Chickpeas).
*   **Low-GI Note:** Heavy on the peppers and spinach for micronutrients and fiber.
*   **Description:** Chicken and chickpeas seasoned with smoked paprika and Aleppo pepper. Fresh spinach is wilted in at the last second.

## Option 5: Golden Chicken & Root Veggie Mash-Up 🥕
*   **Theme:** Rustic & Filling
*   **Composition:** 50% Veg (Carrots, Celery, Onions, Radishes), 25% Protein (Chicken), 25% Starch (Chickpeas).
*   **Low-GI Note:** Uses radishes and celery to bulk up the "mirepoix" volume.
*   **Description:** Sautéed chicken and chickpeas in a savory broth made with bouillon, bulked out with a massive amount of diced root veggies and celery.
