### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 cup Red Lentils (rinsed)
- [ ] 1 medium Yellow Onion (diced)
- [ ] 2-3 cloves Garlic (minced)
- [ ] 2 Carrots (peeled and chopped)
- [ ] 1 Russet Potato (small dice)
- [ ] 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
- [ ] 6 cups Vegetable Broth (or water + bouillon)
- [ ] 1 tsp Cumin
- [ ] 1/2 tsp Coriander
- [ ] 2 tsp Aleppo Pepper (or Paprika + pinch of Cayenne)
- [ ] 1 large Lemon (cut into wedges)
- [ ] 5 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- [ ] Kosher Salt

### Flavor Layers
1. **The Bloom:** Heat olive oil, sautÃ© onion, garlic, carrots, and potato until soft.
2. **The Umami:** Stir in tomato paste and spices (cumin, coriander, half the Aleppo) to coat and toast.
3. **The Simmer:** Add broth and lentils. Simmer 20m until lentils disintegrate.
4. **The Texture:** Blend until creamy.
5. **The Finish:** Sizzling oil with remaining Aleppo pepper and minced garlic drizzled over the top with a heavy lemon squeeze.

### Kid Submeal
- Before blending, fish out several chunks of potato and carrot. Serve plain with a side of cheese toast or pita.
