### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cans Chickpeas (rinsed and roasted or pan-charred)
- [ ] 1 cup Quinoa or Couscous (cooked)
- [ ] **Freezer Stock:** Fire Roasted Roots (thawed and re-seared)
- [ ] 1 bag Spinach (fresh base)
- [ ] 2 Persian Cucumbers (diced)
- [ ] 2 Vine Tomatoes (diced)
- [ ] 1/4 Red Onion (thinly sliced)
- [ ] 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt (for dressing)
- [ ] 1 bunch Fresh Dill (chopped)
- [ ] 1 bunch Fresh Mint (chopped)
- [ ] 1 Lemon (juice and zest)
- [ ] 1 clove Garlic (minced for yogurt sauce)
- [ ] Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- [ ] Salt/Pepper

### Flavor Layers
1. **The Crunch:** Roast chickpeas with cumin and salt until crispy.
2. **The Freshness:** "Shirazi-style" dice for the cucumber and tomato with mint and lime/lemon.
3. **The Cream:** Greek yogurt mixed with heavy dill, lemon zest, and minced garlic.
4. **The Contrast:** Cold spinach and salad against warm roasted roots and crispy chickpeas.

### Kid Submeal
- "Rainbow Plate": Separate piles of chickpeas, roasted roots, cucumber slices, and a dollop of yogurt for dipping.
