## Velveted Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry ðŸ¥¦

*Tender beef without the braise. Ready in 35 mins.*

## Ingredients

- 1 lb Chuck Stew Meat (sliced 1/8" thin against grain).
- **The Velvet:** 1/2 tsp Baking Soda, 1 tbsp Cornstarch, 1 tbsp Soy Sauce.
- 2 large heads Broccoli (small bite-sized florets), 1 bunch Scallions (sliced).
- 1 tbsp Fresh Ginger (grated), 3 cloves Garlic (minced).
- **The Sauce:** 1/2 cup Soy Sauce, 1 tbsp Sesame Oil, 1 tbsp Brown Sugar (or Honey), 1 tsp Cornstarch.
- 2 cups Rice (cooked), Neutral Oil.

## Execution Steps

1. **Velvet (20m):** Toss thin beef slices with "Velvet" ingredients. Let sit 20m. Start rice now.
2. **The Sear (5m):** Heat oil in wok until smoking. Sear beef in two batches (hard char). Remove. **(REMEMBER: Save kid beef strips now!)**
3. **The Veg (4m):** Stir-fry broccoli with a splash of water (cover briefly to steam) until bright green.
4. **The Finish (2m):** Return beef to pan. Add ginger, garlic, and scallions. Toss 30s.
5. **Sauce:** Pour in mixed sauce. Toss until glossy and thick.
