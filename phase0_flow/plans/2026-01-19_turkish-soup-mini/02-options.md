## Option 3: The "Comfort Classics" üè°

*Simple textures, optimized for timing.*

-   **Monday:** **Turkish Lentil Soup.** (35 mins).
    -   *Side:* **Air-Fried/High-Heat Brussels Sprouts** (Halved for speed).
    -   *Kids:* Soup + grilled cheese.
-   **Tuesday:** **Fish & "Chips".** (40 mins).
    -   *Speed Hack:* Cut potatoes into *thin* fries/wedges to roast in 25 mins @ 425F.
    -   *Veg:* **Steamed Green Beans** (5 mins).
    -   *Kids:* Fish sticks (pan fried) + fries + beans.
-   **Thursday:** **Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry.** (30 mins).
    -   *Technique:* Slice stew meat thin against grain. Velveting (baking soda) optional for tenderness.
    -   *Veg:* **Broccoli** (Double volume - half steamed, half stir-fried).
    -   *Kids:* Beef strips + rice + broccoli trees.

**Key New Ingredients:** Red Lentils, Brussels Sprouts, Potatoes, Green Beans, Broccoli.

*Plant-based proteins are often the quickest.*

    -   *Side:* **Massaged Kale Salad**.
    -   *Kids:* Soup + yogurt.
-   **Tuesday:** **Mediterranean Bowl.** (20 mins).

**Key New Ingredients:** Red Lentils, Kale, Chickpeas, Cucumber, Tomato, Spinach, Chicken Sausage, Bell Peppers, Zucchini, Sweet Potato.
